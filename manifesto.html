Kudu Labs Manifesto: Liberating Intelligence

Issacar Ndalo, Founder & CEO of Kudu Labs, October 3, 2025

Humanity and creation should move much further and much faster than the current pace. The potential, strength, and resources we already possess allow us to imagine and build a better future — far beyond what we believe is possible.

Artificial Intelligence is the ultimate weapon and resource of our time. It can unlock unimaginable breakthroughs in science, health, engineering, environment, economy, and more. AI is not just a tool — it is a revolution, an industrial age in itself. Its power is of such value that it is our collective duty to make it accessible for everyone, to solve the problems that surround us.

But AI has become a brake on its own development: its financial, energy, and technological costs slow it down. This burden has made AI increasingly closed, concentrated in the hands of a few financial giants. Research is becoming less independent, less diverse. Even the most celebrated startups — often little more than GPT wrappers — may report high revenues (MRR, ARR) but collapse under extreme bills from OpenAI or Anthropic, which themselves depend on Nvidia. And even Nvidia cannot produce enough chips to meet today’s explosive demand.

We must all take part in the revolution of our time — and fully liberate AI. That means solving the problem of cost, once and for all, so industries can invest freely in research, user experience, growth, and data without being held back by fear of unsustainable bills.

Such a world would allow:

  • deadly and incurable diseases to cease being a threat to humanity;
  • research and public health to save billions of lives;
  • the environment to become understandable and manageable, so we can act meaningfully on climate;
  • space, oceans, and deep waters to be explored;
  • the secrets of biology, plants, and species to nourish both our survival and the beauty of this world;
  • global prosperity to become a reality, even in the most remote places.

Kudu Labs’ vision is to Reimagine how intelligence is powered by making AI cheaper to build and to scale.

Being software-first, our mission is simple:

  • understand and learn from real demand;
  • redistribute workloads intelligently across secure, distributed networks;
  • deliver transparency and sovereignty by design, so every actor can trust each allocation.

The result: compute that is cheaper, greener, and more accessible — without waiting for hardware miracles. In this way, every researcher, startup, enterprise, institution, and citizen can fully participate in this revolution. Because why remain trapped in a system that excludes, threatens our future, and delays technological and social progress?

AI should not be the privilege of a few — it should be the platform for humanity’s next breakthroughs.
Kudu Labs exists to unlock that future.
